Detailed Description

Class that takes two Link objects, defines the location and constraint directions of a joint, and specifies standard deviations and maximum allowed distances to calculate the gradient vectors and Hessian matrices that act on each referenced link.

The method AddGradientsAndHessiansToLinks() computes the link specific gradient vectors and Hessian matrices of the constraint and adds them to the gradient vectors and hessian matrices of the referenced links.

Parameters
link1_ptrdefines the first of the two referenced Link objects that are considered.
link2_ptrdefines the second of the two referenced Link objects that are considered.
body12joint1_posepose of the first joint frame relative to the Body of the first link.
body22joint2_posepose of the second joint frame relative to the Body of the second link.
constraint_directionsdefines which of the 6 joint directions are constrained. The first three directions consider the rotation around x, y, and z, while the second three parameters consider the x, y, and z translation.
max_distance_rotationmaximum valid distance in radians for which the constraint is not active.
max_distance_translationmaximum valid distance in meter for which the constraint is not active.
standard_deviation_rotationstandard deviation for the rotational constraint in rad.
standard_deviation_translationstandard deviation for the translational constraint in meter.
